  src/secp256k1_fastderive_impl.h (new file — not in original)                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
  The core of all custom functionality. Contains:                                                                                                                                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                                                     
  1. secp256k1_ecmult_gen_var — variable-time fixed-base scalar multiplication (tweak * G). Identical logic to the standard secp256k1_ecmult_gen but replaces the 32-entry constant-time cmov scan with a direct     
  table index (adds = prec_table[block][abs]), and skips projective blinding. Safe because BIP32 HMAC-derived tweaks are not secret signing keys. Gives ~35% throughput improvement over the original constant-time  
  path.                                                                                                                                                                                                              
  2. secp256k1_ecmult_gen_var support includes — adds #include "ecmult_gen.h" and #include "precomputed_ecmult_gen.h" to access the comb constants and precomputed table.                                            
  3. secp256k1_serialize33_from_gej / secp256k1_serialize33_from_ge — inline helpers to serialize a Jacobian or affine point to 33-byte compressed form without a full secp256k1_ec_pubkey_serialize call.           
  4. secp256k1_fastderive_parent_impl — struct wrapping a secp256k1_gej (parent pubkey in Jacobian coordinates), cached to avoid re-parsing for repeated child derivations.                                          
  5. secp256k1_fast_pubkey_tweak_add_serialize33_impl — single child key: parent + tweak*G, serialized. Uses secp256k1_ecmult_gen_var.                                                                               
  6. secp256k1_fast_pubkey_tweak_add_serialize33_from_parent_impl — same but takes a pre-cached secp256k1_fastderive_parent_impl (avoids re-loading the parent pubkey). Uses secp256k1_ecmult_gen_var.               
  7. secp256k1_fast_pubkey_tweak_add_serialize33_from_parent_batch10_impl — batch of 10 children from a cached parent: computes 10 tweak_i * G with secp256k1_ecmult_gen_var, adds each to the parent Jacobian, then 
  does one batched modular inversion (secp256k1_ge_set_all_gej_var) to convert all 10 results to affine simultaneously, avoiding 9 individual inversions. Serializes all 10.
